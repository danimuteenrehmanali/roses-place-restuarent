🍴 Sunday & Holiday Brunch👇

The Rose Palace Hotel is a 3-star hotel located in the Gulberg area of Lahore, Pakistan. The hotel offers a Sunday and holiday brunch buffet that is popular among locals and tourists alike. The brunch features a variety of Pakistani and continental dishes, as well as a range of desserts and beverages. The buffet is served in the hotel's restaurant, which has a cozy and elegant ambiance. Guests can enjoy their meal while listening to live music performances or watching sports events on the TV screens in the restaurant.

The Sunday and holiday brunch buffet at the Rose Palace Hotel is priced reasonably and is a great option for those who want to indulge in a delicious meal with family and friends. The hotel recommends making a reservation in advance, as the brunch tends to be quite popular and can get busy. Overall, the Rose Palace Hotel's Sunday and holiday brunch is a great way to spend a leisurely afternoon with loved ones while enjoying a delicious meal in a welcoming atmosphere.
